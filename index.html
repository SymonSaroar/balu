  • Embedded Solutions

    MetroScientific develop RTL layout design on Xilinix FPGA board. We help global businesses embedded software solutions for high-tech applications and IoT.

    We provide CAD modeling and simulation using Comsol, Silvaco, QUANTUM Espresso, PSpice, HSpice, LTspice, Ngspice, Xyce, ModelSim, Matlab Simulink, gpvdm, and VASP.

    MetroScientific has expertise in digital design. We provide digital design with Register Transfer Level (RTL) design, Verilog, and VHDL.
